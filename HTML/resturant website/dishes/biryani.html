beef biryani
Beef Biryani is a rich, aromatic rice dish featuring tender, slow-cooked beef layered with fragrant basmati rice and a bold blend of spices like cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, and cumin. Cooked using the traditional dum (steaming) method, each grain of rice absorbs the deep, savory flavors of the meat and masala. Often garnished with fried onions, herbs, and served with raita or salad, beef biryani is a hearty, indulgent meal that celebrates bold South Asian flavors.
