burger

beef burger
A beef burger is a timeless favorite made with a juicy, seasoned ground beef patty grilled or pan-seared to perfection. Served on a soft, toasted bun, it’s typically layered with fresh lettuce, tomato, onions, cheese, and condiments like ketchup, mustard, or mayo. Whether classic or loaded with extras, the beef burger is a satisfying, bold-flavored meal that never goes out of style.
beef smash burger
A beef smash burger is a crave-worthy twist on the classic burger, made by pressing a ball of ground beef flat on a hot griddle to create a crispy, caramelized crust. Thin, juicy, and packed with flavor, the patty is often stacked double or triple and served with melted cheese, pickles, onions, and special sauce on a toasted bun. It’s all about bold flavor, crispy edges, and juicy bites — a true burger lover’s dream.
chicken burger
A chicken burger is a lighter yet equally satisfying alternative to the classic beef burger, featuring a juicy chicken patty that can be grilled, fried, or breaded. Served on a soft bun, it's often topped with fresh lettuce, tomato, pickles, cheese, and sauces like mayo, spicy aioli, or honey mustard. Crispy or tender, the chicken burger offers a delicious balance of flavor and texture in every bite.
